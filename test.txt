Continuous delivery automates the entire software release process
With continuous deployment changes are deployed to a production environment automatically without the approval from a developer

Continuous delivery is sometimes confused with continuous deployment.
Continuous deployment means that every change is automatically deployed to production.
Continuous delivery means that ( you are able to do frequent deployments )

the team ensures every change can be deployed to production but may choose not to do it, usually due to business reasons.
Continuous delivery tools: bamboo (atlassian) and jenkins
Benefits of continuous delivery:
Automate the entire software release process
Improve developer productivity
Find and address bugs
Deliver updates faster
